UX/UI Case Studies

CrITY

A travel app designed for discovering hidden spots in a city

An end-to-end design project where the basic requirements of creating a scavenger hunt gaming app that enables players to hunt for real objects and locations was provided. My role as a designer was to apply the full design thinking process that included defining a problem, analyzing competitors and the market, developing personas, conducting user research and interviews, building the information architecture, wireframing, creating mid to high fidelity prototypes, and conducting usability testings.

Rebel Harvest

A native app designed to help reduce food waste by offering wonky fruits and vegetables

A 10-week team project focusing on user behaviour and UX design for reducing food waste. While the team started off with the general idea of “reducing food waste”, we narrowed the issue down to focusing on designing an app for saving wonky vegetables & fruits, and education on the impact a consumer would have by saving food that would otherwise become waste. My contributions for the project was strategy, competitor analysis, user research, user testing, information architecture, wireframing, prototyping, and illustrations.

Meggaball

Responsive site for a fitness program

A freelance project where client was dissatisfied with the design of his site and hired me as a UX designer to help rethink user flow and the information architecture. My role for the project was to develop a user journey and user persona and to rethink the information architecture for the site. This led to the client hiring me to help work on launching his site.
